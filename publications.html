Research interests

My general area of interest is differential geometry and its applications to mathematical physics and dynamical systems. More specifically, I'm interested in the geometrical aspects of dissipative systems.

Related topics include:

  • Symplectic geometry
  • Contact geometry
  • Poisson geometry
  • Integrable systems
  • Geometric integrators
  • Geometric control theory
